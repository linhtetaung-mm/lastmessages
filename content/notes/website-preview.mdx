So, I'll keep two versions.

The original version—the notes I write myself—will stay in my personal Obsidian vault. I've already created a folder called `LastMessages`, and that will contain my original writing.

The versions you see on this website will most likely be grammar-corrected editions.

Later, I'd also like to build a tiny AI whose only job is to translate those grammatically cursed writings into articles that people can read without vomiting.
