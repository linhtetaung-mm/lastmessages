I have been coming to this one cafe every day for the last three months. It's called Bunny Drop Cafe in Mingalar Mandalay Complex. I ordered an espresso every day and sat there for about seven hours each day, from 10 AM to 5 PM. I normally don't eat lunch.

So, you might ask, what did I do every day for those seven hours? That's a good question because I don't have a definite answer.

Overall, during those three months, I worked on the Smart Card Generation and Management System, recreated my puzzle games and improved their solving algorithms, did things related to my graduate school admissions, documents, and other stuff, made a small website for a friend from Thailand, developed a program about chess art, drank coffee, listened to music, and ate cheesecake.

Yeah, that's all, I guess.

Some of the time also included watching tech talks on YouTube and asking AI what I should do.

For this personal website, it only took one good afternoon with Codex to build, but before that, I spent quite a bit of time learning and creating unique web designs in Figma. The background color is the same, but the previous design had more elements.

Long story short, it might appear that I wasn't doing anything important during these three months, but I believe in the process. I hope I can be more productive and more useful in the future.

**The End.**
