The best tool is not necessarily the one with the most features. It is the one that leaves the most of your attention available for the work itself.

## Friction is cumulative

A noisy interface asks for a hundred tiny decisions. A calm one has already made the unimportant decisions, and gives you a clear place to begin.

This is why I keep returning to small systems: a text file, a short script, a page with one job. Their limits are not a lack of ambition. They are an invitation to focus.
