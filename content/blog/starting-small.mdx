---
title: Starting small is a technical skill
description: The first version should teach you something before it tries to impress anyone.
date: 2026-05-28
tags: [practice]
---

Small beginnings are easier to revise. They make the next decision visible.

When a project feels too broad, I ask what could be made in an afternoon that would prove the idea deserves a second day. That first artifact is usually enough to reveal the real problem.
